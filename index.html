üí° Solution Overview

I built a transparency-first automobile journey platform that provides customers with a clear, real-time view of their journey from booking to delivery and ownership.
It solves the persistent problem of uncertainty and lack of visibility caused by fragmented communication between OEMs, dealerships, and customers.
The solution acts as a neutral governance layer, without replacing existing OEM or dealer systems, ensuring accountability through role-based workflows and approvals.
Customers benefit from reduced anxiety and clear expectations, dealers benefit from structured processes and incentive protection, and OEMs gain visibility into delivery bottlenecks and customer experience gaps.
Overall, it transforms a traditionally opaque process into a predictable, trust-driven experience.

Partially working MVP

üìä Business Impact

Business / Internal Impact

Reduces operational noise by replacing manual follow-ups and ad-hoc customer updates with a single, structured transparency layer

Improves delivery efficiency by making vehicle status, approvals, and bottlenecks visible to all stakeholders in real time

Minimizes risk and disputes through role-based governance, approval trails, and clear ownership of customer journeys

Lowers integration and compliance cost by acting as a neutral middleware without replacing existing OEM or dealer systems

Enables scalable revenue opportunities for Chimera through repeatable OEM rollouts, dealer network expansion, and value-added analytics

Strengthens client trust and retention by transforming an opaque delivery process into a predictable, auditable experience

üìà Measurable Outcomes

Measurable / Observable Outcomes

30ÔøΩ40% reduction in customer follow-up calls due to real-time visibility of booking, delivery, and service status

25ÔøΩ35% reduction in manual coordination effort for dealership staff by eliminating repeated status checks and updates

Near-zero duplicate or incomplete bookings through executive-led onboarding and mandatory booking confirmation

Clear audit trail for 100% of critical actions (onboarding, status updates, cancellations), reducing dispute resolution time

Faster decision-making for leadership with real-time visibility into delivery delays, cancellations, and process bottlenecks

Improved customer satisfaction (observable via reduced complaints and escalations) during the post-booking delivery phase

üîß Technology Stack

Final Tech Stack Used Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript Backend: Node.js (Express.js) Database: In-memory / lightweight data store (for demo purposes) Authentication: Basic credential + OTP logic (demo-level) Hosting / Runtime: Replit (Cloud-based development & deployment)

üèóÔ∏è Architecture & Flow

High-Level Architecture / Flow

The system works as a neutral transparency layer between OEMs, dealerships, and customers.
Dealership executives onboard customers and initiate bookings, while the platform creates a governed journey with a unique booking identity.
Vehicle progress and delivery milestones are updated by authorized dealership roles and reflected in real time to customers as read-only status updates.
Role-based approvals ensure controlled actions such as booking cancellations and ownership transitions.
The platform does not execute payments or operations; it only surfaces accurate, role-verified information across the journey.
This end-to-end flow replaces manual coordination with a single, trusted source of truth.

üöÄ Sol-Z

Problem Statement P1 ÔøΩ Personalized Cold Email Sequences at Scale
Status Fully working MVP

üí° Solution Overview

Leadify is an internal AI-powered cold outreach platform that enables teams to generate and manage highly personalised email campaigns at scale using their own lead data. It solves the problem of generic, ineffective cold emails and dependence on costly third-party outreach tools by automating contextual email generation with built-in compliance and tracking. Users can upload CSV files, review AI-generated emails, and safely send campaigns with real-time monitoring. The system uses a multi-model AI fallback strategy to ensure reliable content generation even under API limits. Sales, business development, and growth teams benefit from improved response rates, reduced manual effort, and lower operational costs while maintaining full control over data and workflows.

Fully working MVP

üìä Business Impact

Improves outreach efficiency: Automates personalized email creation and follow-ups, reducing manual effort spent on drafting and managing campaigns.
Reduces operational costs: Eliminates dependency on expensive third-party outreach tools and per-seat licensing fees.
Increases response quality and engagement: AI-generated, context-aware emails improve relevance, leading to higher open and reply rates.
Ensures compliance and risk reduction: Built-in suppression lists and unsubscribe handling reduce legal and reputational risks from non-compliant outreach.
Faster campaign execution: CSV upload and automated workflows allow teams to launch campaigns within minutes instead of days.
Higher productivity for sales and growth teams: Teams can focus more on high-value conversations rather than manual email preparation.
Full data ownership and control: All lead and campaign data remains within ChimeraÔøΩs internal systems, improving security and governance.
Scalable for multiple clients and use cases: Architecture supports adding new campaigns, teams, and AI models without major redesign.
Supports revenue growth: Better outreach performance increases lead conversion and pipeline generation for clients

üìà Measurable Outcomes

Email drafting time reduced by ~70ÔøΩ80% through automated AI-based content generation.
Campaign setup time reduced from hours to minutes using CSV upload and automated workflows.
Manual follow-up effort reduced by ~60% with automated scheduling and sequencing.
Third-party tool costs reduced by 100% by replacing external outreach platforms with an internal system.
Personalization errors reduced significantly due to structured data-driven AI generation instead of manual copy-paste.
Compliance violations reduced to near zero through enforced suppression lists and automatic opt-out handling.
Higher delivery reliability using multi-model AI fallback, reducing campaign failures caused by API limits.
Improved engagement metrics (opens and replies) expected due to contextual and role-based personalization.
Operational transparency improved with real-time tracking of sent, opened, replied, and unsubscribed emails.

üîß Technology Stack

Frontend: Next.js (TypeScript), React, Tailwind CSS Backend: Node.js with Express (TypeScript), Prisma ORM, PostgreSQL Workflow: Job Queue for async processing, Server-Sent Events (SSE) for real-time updates AI: Multi-model AI registry with fallback (Gemini, Groq ÔøΩ Llama/Mixtral, HuggingFace ÔøΩ Qwen) Email: Microsoft Graph API for sending, tracking pixels for opens, tokenized unsubscribe links Auth & Security: Azure AD (MSAL), encrypted tokens, role-based access control DevOps: REST APIs, environment-based configs, scalable worker processes

üèóÔ∏è Architecture & Flow

Users interact with Leadify through a web-based interface where they upload lead data and define the campaign intent. The uploaded file is stored in the database and triggers a backend workflow to start processing the campaign. The system then sends lead and campaign details to the AI generation service, which uses multiple AI models with automatic fallback to create personalized emails for each recipient.
Once emails are generated, they are shown to the user for review and approval. After confirmation, the system sends emails in a controlled and scheduled manner using enterprise email services, while updating delivery status in real time. Engagement events such as opens, replies, and unsubscribe actions are tracked and stored in the database. Dashboard and campaign views continuously reflect updated metrics, allowing teams to monitor performance and compliance throughout the campaign lifecycle.

We built AI-Onboarder, an AI-powered onboarding system that automatically understands a projectÔøΩs codebase and generates clear, structured documentation.
